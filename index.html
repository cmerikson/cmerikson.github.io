Christian M. Erikson

I am a geomorphologist soon beginning a postdoc at GFZ Helmholtz. I enjoy studying fluids and fluvial processes. My research often integrates field-based studies and computational methods applied to questions ranging from grain-scale sediment transport to watershed scale hydrological processes.
